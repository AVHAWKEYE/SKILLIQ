📚 How to Acquire GATE Skills
⚡ Engineering Mathematics
  • NPTEL Engineering Mathematics course (IIT level, free) — 12 weeks
  • Solve last 10 years GATE Math questions on GATE Overflow
  • Focus: Linear Algebra (eigen values), Probability (Bayes, distributions)
  • Book: Higher Engineering Mathematics by B.S. Grewal
📊 DSA + Algorithms
  • Complete CLRS Introduction to Algorithms (core chapters)
  • Practice 200+ problems on GeeksforGeeks GATE tag
  • NPTEL DSA course by IIT Delhi (free, certificate available)
  • Solve GATE PYQs 2015-2024 on all DS + Algorithm topics
🧪 Theory of Computation
  • Michael Sipser — Introduction to the Theory of Computation
  • NPTEL TOC course by IIT Chennai — highly rated
  • Practice: DFA minimization, PDA, Turing machines from GATE PYQs
  • GATE Overflow discussions for TOC are excellent reference

📚 How to Acquire Government Job Skills
📊 Data Structures & Algorithms (GATE Focus)
  • Solve 200+ problems on GeeksforGeeks with GATE tag filter
  • Study CLRS or Narasimha Karumanchi DSA book (Chapter by chapter)
  • Take free NPTEL DSA course (IIT certified, 12 weeks)
  • Solve previous 10-year GATE CSE papers on DS + Algo topics
🧮 Quantitative Aptitude & Reasoning
  • RS Aggarwal Quantitative Aptitude — 1 chapter daily (45 days plan)
  • Testbook or Unacademy SSC/GATE mock tests weekly
  • Focus areas: Number theory, ratios, percentages, syllogisms, series
📰 Current Affairs & General Knowledge
  • Read The Hindu / PIB.gov.in daily — 20 minutes morning routine
  • Monthly current affairs PDFs: Vision IAS, Drishti IAS (free download)
  • Weekly quiz on PrepLadder or Testbook for retention testing

Study Abroad (Higher Studies)

MS CS · MS Data Science · PhD · Research · USA / UK / Germany / Canada / Australia

🌟 All Required Skills & Tests for Study Abroad
📝 IELTS / TOEFL🎯 GRE (for USA) 📄 SOP Writing🔬 Research Methodology 📊 Data Structures⚡ Algorithms 🗃 DBMS💬 Academic English 🐘 Python / R🤝 Teamwork & Collaboration
🗣️ Languages Required for Studying Abroad
🇺🇸 English
Primary language in USA, UK, Canada, Australia. Mandatory for admission and daily communication.
REQUIRED — IELTS/TOEFL
🇩🇪 German
Required for German public universities (free tuition). TestDaF or DSH exam for German-medium programs.
REQUIRED (Germany)
🇫🇷 French
Needed for French-medium programs in France, Belgium, Switzerland, Quebec Canada.
Helpful (France/Canada)
🇯🇵 Japanese
Japan MEXT scholarships available. JLPT N3 level helpful for daily life and research programs.
Helpful (Japan)
🇳🇱 Dutch / 🇸🇪 Swedish
Netherlands and Scandinavia offer English programs. Local language helps with daily life.
Helpful (NL/Nordic)
🇰🇷 Korean / 🇦🇪 Arabic
South Korea KGSP and UAE scholarships have growing CSE programs with local language advantages.
Helpful (Korea/UAE)
📚 How to Acquire Study Abroad Skills
📝 IELTS / TOEFL Preparation
  • Target: IELTS 7.0+ overall or TOEFL iBT 100+ for top universities
  • Magoosh TOEFL or British Council IELTS prep course (3 months intensive)
  • Practice 1 full mock test per week; focus on Academic Writing Task 2
  • Speaking: Record daily; summarize TED talks in 2 minutes
🎯 GRE Preparation (USA)
  • Target: 320+ Quant+Verbal, AWA 4.0+ for competitive CS programs
  • Magoosh GRE or ETS Official Prep (3-4 months, 2 hours/day)
  • Quant: Strong for CSE — focus on Data Interpretation + Algebra
  • Verbal: Learn 500 high-frequency GRE words using Anki flashcards
📄 SOP / LOR Writing Strategy
  • SOP structure: Research fit → Academic background → Projects → Career goals
  • Get reviews from seniors admitted to target universities
  • LOR: Request from professors who know your project work directly
  • Avoid generic SOPs — tailor each SOP to each university's research focus
