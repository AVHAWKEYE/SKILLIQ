About SkillIQ

A focused platform for CSE profile analysis, skill-gap insights, and action-oriented planning.

SkillIQ helps learners and administrators evaluate readiness across four career tracks: GATE, Government Jobs, Software/IT Jobs, and Study Abroad. The platform combines document-based analysis, role-aware dashboard views, and downloadable reports.

Built for practical outcomes, SkillIQ highlights what you already know, what is missing, and the exact next steps to improve with measurable progress.
